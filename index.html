I am currently an MRes student of Analytics & Operations at Imperial College Business School. Prior to this, I obtained both my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in School of Mathematics and Statistics at the Beijing Institute of Technology, where I delved into the applications of geometry and topology in data science and biological engineering. I was also a member of the Beijing Key Lab on Mathematical Characterization, Analysis, and Applications of Complex Information(MCAACL). When I was an undergraduate, I had an exchange experience at School of Arts and Science in Rutgers University from September 2018 to March 2019.

My current interests flip over robust optimization, machine learning, high dimensional statistics, and their various applications in data science.
